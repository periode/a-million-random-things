A Million Random Digits with 100,000 Normal Deviates

Audiobook Edition

Disclaimer: since the original book by the RAND Corporation is considered creative work, it is not in the public domain. This audio book is a critical re-invention of the original concept (referred to by some as fan-fiction), based on numbers generated by Nathan Kennedy.

Once upon a time, mankind did not have the computing power to reliably output random digits. Thanks to RAND corporation's patience, creativity and excessive funding, a helpful handbook was published in nineteen fifty five, to the relief of statisticians, physicists, polltakers, market analysts, lottery administrators and quality control engineers.
